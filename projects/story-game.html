Online Adventure Game

Computer Science / Technology

Objectives

1. To understand basic logic in programming and conditionals.

2. Apply learning and skills

Summary

Students apply the knowledge they've learned about JavaScript conditionals and Document Object Model (DOM) manipulation. The goal was to create a game online that in the theme of a "choose your own adventure". Some students decided to create stories from old children's books, horror, high school life, and projects modeled off real life selection games.

The project itself was designed to make learning of conditional logic interesting and allow students to be creative with their learning. Click the images below to see some example projects made by students.
